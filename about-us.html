About Wood Works LLC – Our History, Locations & Approach

We manufacture and deliver the high-quality wood products you can count on every day.

Company Overview

Founded in 1918, Timber Products produces a diversified range of wood products. Best known for its hardwood plywood, the company is committed to environmental sustainability and offers a fully integrated approach to manufacturing with 8 manufacturing facilities, an import division, and a nationwide logistics and transportation division.

Vision

To deliver the best customer experience in the wood products and logistics industry.

Mission

Work together to provide integrated wood products and logistics solutions to our customers.

Values

Do the right thing, be accountable, and compete

Leadership

Woodwork is a family-owned business with four generations of history.

Major Markets

Wood work is one of the largest material suppliers to the nation’s kitchen/bath cabinet, furniture, store fixture/display and architectural millwork industries.

Natural Resources

We source from forestlands in Northern California and Southern Oregon.

Other Services

TP Logistics, a national third-party logistics service provider for a wide range of products and industries. TP Trucking, a national trucking service that transports a wide range of products, including the company’s wood products. On-site consulting services with manufacturers of cabinetry, furniture, fixtures and millwork.

Employees

Over 1,200 full-time employees nationwide.

PRODUCTS FOR EVERY NEED

We specialize in hardwood plywood and wood panels with decorative overlays. From veneer to ultralight MDF to particleboard, we offer an incredible variety of products. If you don’t see exactly what you need, we can custom manufacture certain products!

RESPONSIBLE MANUFACTURING

All of our products are responsibly created with the environment in mind. We continually improve processes to reduce waste. We use no-added formaldehyde (NAF) based resins or ultra-low emitting formaldehyde (ULEF) resins to reduce emissions. Our RhinoCoat prefinished panels include an antimicrobial additive that protects the panels from degradation and decay.
